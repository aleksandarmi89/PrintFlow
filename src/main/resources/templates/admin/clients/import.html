📝 Import Tips

  • Required columns: Company Name, Contact Person, and Phone must be filled for each row.
  • Email uniqueness: If an email already exists in the system, that row will be skipped.
  • Large files: For files with more than 1000 rows, consider splitting them into smaller batches.
  • Data validation: Phone numbers should be in international format (e.g., +381 11 123 4567).
